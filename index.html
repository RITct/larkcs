About Us

In a fast spinning world, it’s not always possible to be with and take care of someone who needs a health related assistance. You would be facing such dilemmas in your busy lives because of many reasons. The very next time when you are in such a situation where you cannot lend a hand of love and care to someone in need, borrow ours! We will feel your pulse and pull up your smile to make you feel at home.

Who we are

LARKCS Life Link is a visionary foundation embarking a new journey in the history of medical hospitality. We couldn't turn a blind eye towards the pain and helplessness the people around us face each day in health related problems. And that generous thought laid the milestone of Larkcs Life Link.


Our mission is to provide a complete health assistance to our customers, for a lifetime.

What we do

We would guide you through the confusing maze of alleys in health centers. We will save your time by doing all the necessary office procedures for you. We would also do the time-consuming and tedious tasks like collecting medicines, equipments and test results to even taking the simple token for consultation! And all the while, you can take rest and relax in our private lounges available in the hospitals we are working. The bystanders provided by us would always be with you as a family member if you are staying in the medical center for a long time.

In a nutshell, you will be surrounded by an aura of love and care of LARKCS Life Link endlessly.

Our Services

To make things even easier, you can order our service from home through your smartphone, the website or by direct call. We will even come and get from your home to the hospitals and back if you want us to.

Runner

A runner is best suitable for short services lasting only for a matter of hours. Best in case of consultation and checkups.

Bystander

A bystander is suitable for long services lasting across days. Best in case of admittance. The bystanders provided by us would always be with you as a family member if you are staying in the medical center for a long time.
